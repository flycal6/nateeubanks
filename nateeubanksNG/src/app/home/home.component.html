Nate Eubanks

@NateEubanks

Nate Eubanks grew up in rural Northeast Kansas outside of a town of just over 3,000. He has performed stand-up comedy in 12 countries: Canada, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, South Africa, Netherlands, Australia, Thailand, Georgia, Oman, Russia and through the midwest USA. From 5 star hotels in Qatar, art galleries in Johannesburg, garages in Chicago, clubs in Sydney, and bars in Tbilisi Nate brings the laughs to every audience.
