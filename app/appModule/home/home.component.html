Nate Eubanks grew up in rural Northeast Kansas and later worked internationally in Paraguay and Kuwait. He has performed stand up comedy in eight countries: Canada, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, South Africa, Netherlands, Australia and through the midwest USA. From 5 star hotels in Qatar, art galleries in Johannesburg, garages in Chicago, and finally clubs in Sydney, Nate brings the laughs to every audience.
